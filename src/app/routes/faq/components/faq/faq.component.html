Q1. Unable to login to Lex

For using Lex you have to register yourself for Multi-Factor Authentication.
Please login to Lex from https://lex.infosysapps.com
Lex is an internet facing application there are two scenarios.
1. You are accessing Lex on Infosys Intranet (VPN yes but not IConnect)
2. You are accessing Lex on Internet (from your personal laptop/desktop/Mobile browser/Android/iOS native applications)
In 1) you will not have to do separate authentication, your windows login will be automatically used for letting you in Lex
In 2) You need to register yourself first for Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) at https://rasssp.ad.infosys.com/MultiFactorAuth/ on intranet and register.
The passcode or pin you set up here will be used for login later.
Once you enter your Infosys user name and password on a Microsoft Page, you will receive a call prompting you to use the passcode/pin to authenticate yourself.
You can also either register or change your pin if you have forgotten.

Q3. Login credentials for Lex

Please visit:
 https://rasssp.ad.infosys.com/MultiFactorAuth/
 and register yourself for Multi-Factors Authentication.
You can set a passcode of your choice. Select Call-In method for authentication.
For accessing Lex this is a “must” criteria.
1. Visit https://lex.infosysapps.com
2. Press Login Button
3. If you are on Infosys Network, you will not have to just enter your Infosys Mail ID
4. If you are on Internet, on button click, you will be taken to Microsoft Login Site. Please enter your Infosys Mail ID
5. You will be taken to stsakaash site which is from Infosys. Here you provide your email id and password(Infosys account)
6. You will receive a phone call from Microsoft on your registered phone number
7. You are required to key-in your passcode and #
8. If your authentication is successful, the web browser will automatically take you to Lex.

Q4. Unable to logon to Lex , getting Microsoft Login page

Clear the browser cache and cookies (going as far back as possible).
This issue is mainly for user who use other Infosys O365 Applications like Mail, VPN, Yammer and many more.
The token issued by Microsoft when you used such an application is stored in the cookies and Microsoft needs to be forced to issue a fresh token which will allow access the Lex application which is also a O365 Application.
Clear the cache and cookies and restart the browser and try accessing Lex

Q2. IOS - To Install IOS App

Kindly find the steps enclosed below for your request please.
To install the app, Safari Web browser is required.
Please follow the following steps
1. Go to https://lex.infosysapps.com on Safari browser on your iPhone (This will not work on Google Chrome or other browsers)
2. Login to the app (using MFA)
3. Go to “Mobile Apps” under the ☰
4. Click on IOS tab
5. Click on Download
You will be prompted to Install the app, Please click on install.
Once you accept the installation, the app would start the download process in the background and the AppIcon would be visible on the home screen of your phone
Fix for “Untrusted Developer” Error (Comes up if Lex is the first app installed from “Infosys Limited”)
To fix this, you need to Trust the Developer “Infosys Limited”. Setting to trust the app are as follows
1. Go to Device Management in your iPhone (Settings > General > Device Management)
2. Click on “Infosys Limited” Under the Enterprise Apps
3. Click on “Trust”
4. Re-launch the app.
Mobile app needs your one-time Login after the first launch.

Q2. Could you please guide me on how to record a video in LEX with real time voice over.

Lex does not have the capabilities to record videos or author PDF or HTML Content directly on its authoring tool.
If you have created Videos either using screen-capture using tools like Captivate, Camtasia or converting PPTs to Videos with voiceover or even using a video recording device like Phones, Video Cameras, Lex allows you to create courses out of such content.
Course creation involves providing a good amount of meta-data for non-text based contents such as Videos.
